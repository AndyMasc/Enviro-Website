About Me

I’m Andy — a student and programmer passionate about designing practical, affordable technology. From building 3D-printed prosthetic hands to coding embedded systems and AI-powered tools, I focus on creating solutions that blend innovation with impact. This portfolio showcases my projects, experiments, and ideas that bridge hardware, software, and human purpose.

My Catholic Perspective

As a Catholic and New Zealander, I believe caring for creation is part of our duty to God. Pope Benedict XVI called Earth our “common home,” and Pope Francis in Laudato Si reminds us that protecting it is a moral act. Climate change matters to me because our environment is a gift from God, and we’re called to protect it for future generations.
